Platform

A delivery engine for deployable optimization and control work

Akreon’s platform is how applied engineering work becomes faster to deliver, easier to harden, and more reusable across repeated customer problems while Akreon retains ownership of the core technology.

The hard part is rarely just the algorithm or just the deployment target. It is the interface between them: how constrained control, MPC, and synthesis workflows become deployable software, reviewable artifacts, and production-oriented implementations for real systems.

Akreon uses its proprietary platform to support results-as-a-service delivery. Depending on the engagement, customers may receive direct engineering execution, deployable builds, solver or control artifacts, embedded integration support, and, where it fits, licensed platform-backed usage.

Platform focus

Platform-backed delivery

The platform helps Akreon move from scoped technical problem to delivered implementation faster, with less reinvention and stronger deployment discipline.

Deployable technical artifacts

Deliverables can include solver and control builds, production-oriented implementations, and engineering outputs designed to survive timing, operating, and embedded constraints, with rights defined by the engagement.

Reusable infrastructure over time

Ground-up deterministic implementation, modern C++23, and compile-time-oriented design support work that can move from one-off delivery into reusable Akreon platform capability when the fit is strong.

Delivery model

Engagements can start as pilot projects, forward-deployed technical work, or embedded optimization and control delivery. Customers receive defined deliverables and deployment rights per engagement, while Akreon retains ownership of its core platform and technology.
