Technical Infrastructure

Infrastructure for model-based decision systems.

This page is kept available for URL continuity. Akreon's current public focus is model-based decision systems for constrained, uncertain environments.

Akreon develops software and methods that connect models, measurements, objectives, and constraints to repeatable actions. The work is grounded in optimization, control, estimation, simulation, and implementation-aware numerical software.

Technical scope

Representative areas of infrastructure and method development.

Problem structure

  • Models and measurements
  • Objectives and tradeoffs
  • Constraints and operating limits
  • Uncertainty and timing requirements

Decision layers

  • Feedback control and estimation
  • Safety filters and constrained updates
  • Trajectory optimization and MPC
  • Planning, allocation, and higher-level policies
