Platform

A delivery engine for real control software

Akreon helps teams move from feedback control, estimation, constrained control, and synthesis workflows to deployable systems.

The hard part is the interface between design and deployment: deterministic software, reviewable artifacts, embedded constraints, validation, and real operation.

The platform backs pilot delivery, forward-deployed technical execution, and repeatable workflows. Adjacent expansion areas include trajectory optimization, MPC, learned-control workflows, and system identification where they strengthen the deployable control stack.

Platform focus

Feedback and estimation first

Initial workflows center on controller design, observer design, synthesis, robustness, and deployment readiness.

Constrained workflows

Near-term platform areas include CLF-CBF QPs, reference governors, safety filters, gain scheduling, and constrained control.

Reviewable artifacts

Deliverables can include builds, generated C/C++ artifacts, integration support, and validation-conscious technical outputs.

Delivery model

Engagements can start as pilots, forward-deployed technical work, or embedded control delivery. Deliverables and deployment rights are defined per engagement; Akreon retains its core platform technology.
