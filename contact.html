Work with Akreon

Focused technical work on difficult decision systems.

Akreon is a technical lab and consulting practice that works with teams and technical leaders on constrained decision problems involving uncertainty, feedback, simulation, optimization, estimation, autonomy architecture, and implementation limits.

Engagement Paths

Ways to engage

Technical advisory

Focused review of controllers, estimators, optimization formulations, autonomy architectures, ROS 2 system design, or simulation-to-deployment plans.

Consulting / prototype support

Scoped work to turn a decision problem into a model, prototype, architecture, or deployable technical artifact.

Robotics & autonomy architecture

ROS 2, mapping, estimation, navigation, planning, control, and integration across decision layers.

Research collaboration

Joint investigation of control, optimization, estimation, robustness, simulation, or quantitative decision systems.

Content / tutorial discussion

Questions, corrections, extensions, or collaborations related to Akreon's public technical work.

Common Starting Points

A focused place to begin

  • 60–90 minute technical review
  • Architecture or formulation audit
  • Prototype feasibility sprint
  • ROS 2 / autonomy stack review
  • Robust control, MPC, estimation, or optimization review
  • Simulation-to-deployment planning

Good fit

Good fits include problems where models, constraints, uncertainty, and action meet: robotics and autonomy architecture, ROS 2 systems, mapping, estimation, navigation, planning, controls, optimization, industrial operations, and simulation-to-deployment workflows.

Not a fit

Generic app development, dashboards-only work, vague AI automation requests, or projects that do not involve modeling, optimization, uncertainty, autonomy, or decision-making.
