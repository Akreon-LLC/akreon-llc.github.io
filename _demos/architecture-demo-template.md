## Problem

State the operational or technical problem being addressed. Keep this concrete.

## Setup

Describe the system context, environment, assumptions, and constraints.

## Method

Summarize the approach without over-disclosing proprietary details.

## Results

Present the relevant outputs, metrics, or observed behavior.

## Why It Matters

Explain what the result demonstrates about deployment readiness, architecture quality, or system behavior.
