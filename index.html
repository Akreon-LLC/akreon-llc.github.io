Control and estimation delivery

Most teams can design a controller. Few can deploy it.

Control and estimation methods often work in simulation. Akreon helps teams turn them into deterministic, reviewable software that survives embedded constraints, validation, and real operation.

Engagements can produce builds, reviewable artifacts, integration support, and licensed platform components where appropriate.

Platform core

Akreon’s platform turns control design, estimation, optimization, and synthesis workflows into software teams can review, validate, and reuse.

Delivery

Scoped engagements move from deployment problem to implementation, build, and defined deployment rights.

Control execution

Akreon delivers feedback control, observer design, robust synthesis, constrained control, gain scheduling, and deployment workflows shaped around timing, limits, and embedded constraints.

Reusable artifacts

Deterministic C++23 and compile-time-oriented design support artifacts that are reviewable, validation-conscious, and reusable.

Technical workflows

Initial workflows cover state feedback, observer design, polytopic LMI synthesis, MIMO PID optimization, H₂/H∞ synthesis, H∞ loop shaping, robust synthesis objects, CLF-CBF QPs, and reference governors. Trajectory optimization and MPC are next.

Published artifacts

Akreon publishes notebooks, HTML writeups, white papers, and examples that connect design and analysis to deployable control software.
