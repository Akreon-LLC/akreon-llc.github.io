About

An engineering-led decision systems company.

Akreon builds at the decision layer, where models, measurements, objectives, and constraints become action.

The company's foundation comes from GN&C, robotics, autonomy, control, estimation, and optimization: fields where uncertainty, nonlinear dynamics, timing limits, and physical consequences are not edge cases. They are the problem.

Founder David Akre's background spans flight software and GN&C for deep-space and CubeSat-class systems, autonomous vehicles, robotics, and sensor fusion.

Akreon's work focuses on principled software and methods for constrained, real-world decision systems.
