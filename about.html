About

An engineering-led control software company.

Akreon helps controls teams move from synthesis and simulation toward generated software that can be reviewed, built, and integrated.

The current product wedge is feedback control and estimation, with structured optimization pilots and runtime optimization roadmap work kept distinct.

The company is technical by default: explicit interfaces, restrained claims, and outputs that embedded teams can inspect.

Operating principles

Controls-native

Workflows start from models, constraints, objectives, synthesis settings, and target requirements.

Deployment-focused

Generated code, headers, metadata, build outputs, and integration boundaries are product outputs.

Reviewable

Outputs are intended to fit existing verification, test, and embedded software processes.

Founder

David Akre

Founder of Akreon. Focused on control software, constrained optimization, synthesis workflows, and embedded deployment.
