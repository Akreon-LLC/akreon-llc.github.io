About

An engineering-led control software company

Akreon helps teams turn control and estimation work into deployable systems.

We focus on the layer where controller design, software architecture, and deployed systems meet: implementations, artifacts, embedded integration, and fieldable capability.

Our posture is straightforward: stay technical, stay selective, and compound direct engineering work into durable platform capability.

The initial customer wedge is robotics, autonomy, mobility, industrial vehicles, and mission systems that need feedback control, estimation, constrained control, and synthesis workflows.

How we work

Results-first execution

We start from the customer’s technical bottleneck and work toward implementation, build, and operational output.

Platform-backed leverage

We use proprietary deterministic infrastructure to make repeated delivery faster, harder to break, and more reusable.

Selective technical fit

We work with teams whose deployment problems align with Akreon’s technical direction.
