Applications

Where platform-backed delivery is a strong fit

Akreon is focused on domains where customers need applied optimization and control execution, deployable artifacts, and durable infrastructure rather than abstract tooling alone.

Robotics and autonomous systems

Programs that need deployable MPC, constrained control, and working software artifacts across sensing, planning, control, and runtime constraints.

Automotive, mobility, off-road, and industrial vehicles

Platforms where timing, operating limits, embedded integration, and control quality determine whether optimization and control work can actually be shipped.

Aerospace, spacecraft, and mission systems

Programs where deterministic behavior, validation discipline, and deployable technical artifacts matter as much as the underlying control logic.

Warehouse and industrial automation

Operational environments where gain scheduling, constrained behavior, embedded deployment, and production-oriented solver or control builds all matter alongside throughput and coordination.

Selected platform-adjacent domains

Some additional domains share the same delivery problems, but the current commercial wedge is autonomy, mobility, and mission-oriented real systems where applied execution is urgent.

These are deployment settings where the same customer need appears: move from hard optimization or control problem to delivered implementation, then, where there is fit, into licensed platform-backed deployment over time.
